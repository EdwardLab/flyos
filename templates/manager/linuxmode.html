Launch Linux Desktop Mode on your device

This mode allows displaying the FlyOS Desktop using X11 on your device via SDL. Compared to VNC, this mode supports GPU and sound, enhancing compatibility for games and similar applications, more realistic computer experience. However, it might not be very user-friendly on a pure touchscreen, so it's recommended to connect a mouse and keyboard for a better and easier experience

Before launch this mode, make sure:
  • Unlock your device (Password, PIN, fingerprint...)

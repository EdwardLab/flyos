What is FlyOS

  1. FlyOS deploys a Linux container (FlyOS is based on Ubuntu) for your Android device, with a visual control panel to manage your container and devices
  2. 100% open source and free, immediately deploy subsystems for your Android devices

System Requirements

  • Android 7.0 or later (Linux Kernel 4.9+ recommended)
  • At least 4GB of RAM recommended
  • At least 15GB of free storage space
  • Full Root access (such as Magisk, etc.)
  • AOSP is recommended, full system and data partition read and write (r/w) permissions
    • Please click Deploy Now to check whether the device meets the installation conditions. If the installation is forced, FlyOS may not work properly

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FlyOS?

FlyOS is a Debian-like operating system customized for Android devices, running through chroot containers, with an easy-to-use panel to manage your containers

What is Chroot Linux container?

Deploy the Linux system FlyOS for your device through the unix-like container "chroot"

Is FlyOS free?

Yes, FlyOS is completely free and open source. Same as 2.0, FlyOS 3.0 is released under the GPL-3.0 License

How do I report bugs or suggest new features?

You can report bugs or suggest new features on our GitHub issue tracker.
